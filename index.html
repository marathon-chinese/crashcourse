About This Program

We have been expecting you...

Te hemos estado esperando...

It our hope that you will enjoy our Chinese class which we made with you in mind! We are motivated by friends like you who endeavour to communicate the word of God without impartiality! So THANK YOU!!!

Before Class

Days leading to the class, we urge to familiarize yourself with the 300 words from the old HSK 1 & 2 or the 500 from the new HSK 1. If you are above these levels you may practise other levels just for fun and you may still attend as our respected reference

Los días previos a la clase, lo instamos a que se familiarice con las 300 palabras del antiguo HSK 1 y 2 o las 500 del nuevo HSK 1. Si está por encima de estos niveles, puede practicar otros niveles solo por diversión y aún puede asistir. como nuestra respetada referencia

During Class

Hit the ground running, do all the exercises. Be the loudest student. Practise with your partner. Send audios of you speaking. Read widely. It's only for a month!

Ponte en marcha, haz todos los ejercicios. Sé el estudiante más ruidoso. Practica con tu compañero. Envía audios de tu discurso. Lee mucho. ¡Es solo por un mes!

After Class

We recommend you keep learning so that you do not forget what you would have learned. Feel free to stop by to ask for further suggestions you may apply.

Te recomendamos que sigas aprendiendo para que no olvides lo que hubieras aprendido, no dudes en pasar a preguntarnos por más sugerencias que puedas aplicar.
